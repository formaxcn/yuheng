# Dashboard & History Tracking

The YuHeng dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your nutritional progress and trends over time.

## Daily Progress
The main dashboard features a **Circular Progress Tracker** that shows your current calorie intake against your daily target.
- **Macro Breakdown**: Real-time progress bars for Protein, Carbs, and Fats.
- **Meal Tiles**: Quick view of consumption categorized by meal type (Breakfast, Lunch, etc.).

## Weekly Trends
The history chart displays your energy consumption for the **last 7 days**, helping you identify patterns and stay consistent with your goals.

## Real-Time Aggregation
All stats are aggregated on-the-fly from your logs. Every time you log a new meal or snack, your dashboard updates immediately to reflect the change.
